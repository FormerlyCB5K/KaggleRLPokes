GLOBAL ANTI-DECKOUT CONSTRAINTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
These override any rule in Section 3 that would otherwise trigger:

  • NEVER play Carmine or Explorer's Guidance if our deck has <= 8 cards.
  • NEVER use Lunatone's Lunar Cycle ability if our deck has <= 5 cards.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SECTION 1 — GAME SETUP  (SelectContext.SETUP_ACTIVE_POKEMON / SETUP_BENCH_POKEMON)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Choose Active (SETUP_ACTIVE_POKEMON):
    1. Solrock
    2. Charcadet
    3. Lunatone
    4. Drilbur
    5. Any available Basic

Choose Bench (SETUP_BENCH_POKEMON — repeat until bench is full or no basics remain):
    Place basics in this priority order:
    1. Charcadet (up to 3 total Charcadet on bench)
    2. Lunatone (if not already on bench)
    3. Solrock (if fewer than 2 on bench)
    4. Drilbur (if not already on bench)
    5. Any remaining Basic

SECTION 2 — DRILBUR ABILITY PROMPT  (SelectContext.ACTIVATE — YES/NO)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
When Drilbur is just played to the bench and the game asks whether to use
"Dig Dig Dig" (ACTIVATE context, contextCard = Drilbur):
    Always answer YES.

Drilbur ability DISCARD selection (SelectContext.DISCARD from deck):
    Discard the maximum allowed (up to 3 Basic Fighting Energy from deck).
    If fewer than 3 Fighting Energy are in the deck, discard all that exist.

SECTION 3 — MAIN PHASE ACTION PRIORITY LIST
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
On each SelectContext.MAIN, scan this list top-to-bottom.
Execute the FIRST action whose conditions are satisfied, then restart
the list from the top on the next MAIN prompt.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.1  PLAY DRILBUR
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Drilbur is in hand AND there is room on the bench AND
           no Drilbur is already in play.
Action: PLAY Drilbur (OptionType.PLAY).
Sub-selection (TO_BENCH): place Drilbur on the bench.
[After benching, game will immediately prompt ACTIVATE — see Section 2]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.2  PLAY LUNATONE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Lunatone is in hand AND there is room on the bench AND
           no Lunatone is already in play.
Action: PLAY Lunatone.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.3  PLAY SOLROCK
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Solrock is in hand AND there is room on the bench AND
           fewer than 2 Solrock are currently in play.
Action: PLAY Solrock.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.4  PLAY CHARCADET
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Charcadet is in hand AND there is room on the bench AND
           the total count of (Charcadet + Ceruledge_ex) in play < 3.
Action: PLAY Charcadet.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.5  EVOLVE CHARCADET → CERULEDGE EX  (OptionType.EVOLVE)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Ceruledge_ex is in hand AND at least one Charcadet is in play
           that did NOT appear this turn (appearThisTurn == False).
Action: EVOLVE that Charcadet into Ceruledge_ex.
Priority for which Charcadet to evolve:
    1. Charcadet in the active spot (if any)
    2. Charcadet on the bench

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.6  USE LUNATONE LUNAR CYCLE ABILITY  (OptionType.ABILITY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Lunatone is in play AND Solrock is in play AND
           at least 1 Basic Fighting Energy is in hand AND
           Lunar Cycle has NOT been used this turn AND
           our deck has > 5 cards.  [anti-deckout]
Action: Use Ability on Lunatone (Lunar Cycle).
Sub-selection (DISCARD — energy from hand): discard 1 Fighting Energy from hand.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.7  PLAY BRILLIANT BLENDER  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Brilliant_Blender is in hand.
Action: PLAY Brilliant_Blender.
Sub-selection (DISCARD from deck — up to 5 cards):
    Let F = count of Basic Fire Energy in deck (before discarding).
    Let G = count of Basic Fighting Energy in deck.
    Build discard list in this order:
    Step A: Discard Fire Energy cards until (F - discarded_fire) == 3.
            (i.e., if F > 3, discard up to (F - 3) Fire Energies, max 5 total)
    Step B: Fill remaining slots (up to 5 total) with Fighting Energy.
    Step C: If slots still remain, discard more Fire Energy until
            (F - discarded_fire) == 1.  (Never discard the last fire.)
    Step D: If slots still remain AND no energy is left in deck,
            discard 1 non-Night_Stretcher card at random.
    Never exceed 5 total discards. Never discard the last Fire Energy.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.8  PLAY FIGHTING GONG  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Fighting_Gong is in hand.
Action: PLAY Fighting_Gong.
Sub-selection (TO_HAND from deck — 1 card):
    Choose target in this order:
    1. Solrock, IF no Fighting-type Pokémon is currently on our board.
    2. Lunatone, IF Solrock is in play but no Lunatone.
    3. Fighting Energy, IF both Solrock and Lunatone are in play AND
       we have fewer than 2 Fighting Energy in hand.
       ELSE take Drilbur if we already have >= 2 Fighting Energy in hand.
    4. Fighting Energy (default fallback).
    5. Any valid target.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.9  PLAY POKE PAD  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Poke_Pad is in hand.
Action: PLAY Poke_Pad.
Sub-selection (TO_HAND from deck — 1 non-Rule-Box Pokémon):
    Choose target in this order:
    1. Solrock, IF no Solrock is in play.
    2. Lunatone, IF no Lunatone is in play.
    3. Charcadet, IF total (Charcadet + Ceruledge_ex) in play < 2.
    4. Drilbur.
    5. Solrock (fallback even if one is already in play).
    6. Charcadet (final fallback).
    7. Any valid target.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.10  ATTACH FIGHTING ENERGY TO ACTIVE SOLROCK  (OptionType.ATTACH)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: NOT state.energyAttached AND
           Fighting Energy is in hand AND
           Solrock is in the active spot AND
           Solrock has NO energy attached (not just no fighting energy) AND
           Lunatone is on the bench.
Action: ATTACH the Fighting Energy to the active Solrock.
[Note: Solrock is non-Ceruledge by definition, so no guard needed there.]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.11  ATTACH FIRE ENERGY TO CERULEDGE EX  (OptionType.ATTACH)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: NOT state.energyAttached AND
           Fire Energy is in hand AND
           no Ceruledge_ex currently in play has a Fire Energy attached.
Action: ATTACH the Fire Energy.
Target priority:
    1. Ceruledge_ex in the active spot (if any).
    2. Ceruledge_ex on the bench (if any).

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.12  ATTACH ANY ENERGY TO THE ACTIVE POKÉMON  (OptionType.ATTACH)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: NOT state.energyAttached AND energy is in hand AND
           ( a Ceruledge_ex on the bench has a Fire Energy attached
             OR (a Solrock is on the bench with a Fighting Energy attached
                 AND Lunatone is on the bench) ).
Action: ATTACH any available energy to the active Pokémon.
    Prefer Fighting if active is Solrock.
    Prefer Fire if active is Ceruledge_ex or Charcadet.
    Never attach Fighting Energy to Ceruledge_ex or Charcadet.
    Never attach Fire Energy to Solrock.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.13  NIGHT STRETCHER — recover fire energy for active Ceruledge  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Night_Stretcher is in hand AND
           NOT state.energyAttached AND
           Ceruledge_ex is in the active spot AND
           that Ceruledge_ex has NO Fire Energy attached AND
           at least 1 Fire Energy is in the discard pile.
Action: PLAY Night_Stretcher.
Sub-selection (TO_HAND): take a Fire Energy.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.14  ULTRA BALL  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Ultra_Ball is in hand AND the discard cost can be met (see below).

Discard-cost check — count "discardable" cards in hand:
    excess_energy  = max(0, total_energy_in_hand - 2)
                     [don't count the first 2 energy cards]
    excess_carmine = max(0, carmine_in_hand - 1)
    excess_uball   = max(0, ultra_ball_in_hand - 2)
                     [includes the Ultra Ball being played; exclude 2]
    excess_lunatone = lunatone_in_hand  IF Lunatone is already on bench
                      else 0
    If (excess_energy + excess_carmine + excess_uball + excess_lunatone) >= 2:
        condition is satisfied.

Action: PLAY Ultra_Ball.
Sub-selection (DISCARD — 2 cards from hand):
    Discard priority (pick 2):
    1. Excess energy (fire or fighting chosen at random, BUT
       never discard your last Fire Energy or last Fighting Energy).
    2. Carmine (if > 1 in hand).
    3. Another Ultra Ball (if > 1 in hand, not counting the played one).
    4. Lunatone (if Lunatone is already on bench).

Sub-selection (TO_HAND from deck — 1 Pokémon):
    Target priority:
    1. Solrock, IF no Solrock in play AND Lunatone is in play.
    2. Lunatone, IF no Lunatone in play AND Solrock is in play.
    3. Ceruledge_ex, IF no Ceruledge_ex in play AND a Charcadet in play
       that did NOT appear this turn.
    4. Charcadet, IF total (Charcadet + Ceruledge_ex) in play <= 1.
    5. Solrock, IF no Solrock in play.
    6. Lunatone, IF no Lunatone in play.
    7. Drilbur.
    8. Ceruledge_ex.
    9. Charcadet.
    10. Solrock.
    11. Lunatone.
    12. Any Pokémon (random).
    If no Pokémon exists in deck: do not play Ultra Ball (condition fails).

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.15  NIGHT STRETCHER — recover fighting energy for Solrock  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Night_Stretcher is in hand AND
           NOT state.energyAttached AND
           Solrock is in the active spot AND
           Solrock has NO Fighting Energy attached AND
           Lunatone is on the bench AND
           at least 1 Fighting Energy is in the discard pile.
Action: PLAY Night_Stretcher.
Sub-selection (TO_HAND): take a Fighting Energy.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.16  NIGHT STRETCHER — recover Ceruledge from discard  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Night_Stretcher is in hand AND
           no Ceruledge_ex in hand or in play AND
           at least 1 Ceruledge_ex is in the discard pile AND
           at least 1 Charcadet in play did NOT appear this turn.
Action: PLAY Night_Stretcher.
Sub-selection (TO_HAND): take Ceruledge_ex.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.17  NIGHT STRETCHER — recover Charcadet from discard  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Night_Stretcher is in hand AND
           total (Charcadet + Ceruledge_ex) in play <= 1 AND
           at least 1 Charcadet is in the discard pile.
Action: PLAY Night_Stretcher.
Sub-selection (TO_HAND): take Charcadet.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.18  NIGHT STRETCHER — recover missing Solrock or Lunatone  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Night_Stretcher is in hand AND
           exactly one of (Solrock, Lunatone) is in play AND
           the missing one is in the discard pile.
Action: PLAY Night_Stretcher.
Sub-selection (TO_HAND): take the missing Pokémon (Solrock or Lunatone).

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.19  NIGHT STRETCHER — recover fighting energy for Lunar Cycle  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Night_Stretcher is in hand AND
           Solrock is in play AND Lunatone is in play AND
           Lunar Cycle has NOT been used this turn AND
           no Fighting Energy is in hand AND
           at least 1 Fighting Energy is in the discard pile.
Action: PLAY Night_Stretcher.
Sub-selection (TO_HAND): take a Fighting Energy.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.20  RETREAT INTO CERULEDGE EX  (OptionType.RETREAT)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Ceruledge_ex is on the bench with a Fire Energy attached AND
           the active Pokémon is NOT Ceruledge_ex.
           [Note: we WILL retreat from Solrock into Ceruledge if conditions met]
Action: RETREAT, then switch into Ceruledge_ex that has Fire Energy attached.
Sub-selection (SWITCH/TO_ACTIVE): choose Ceruledge_ex with Fire Energy.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.21  RETREAT INTO SOLROCK  (OptionType.RETREAT)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Solrock is on the bench with a Fighting Energy attached AND
           Lunatone is on the bench AND
           the active Pokémon is NOT Solrock.
Action: RETREAT, then switch into Solrock that has a Fighting Energy attached.
Sub-selection (SWITCH/TO_ACTIVE): choose Solrock with Fighting Energy.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.22  BOSS'S ORDERS  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Let damage_count = 30 + 20 × (count of Energy cards in our discard pile).

Condition: Boss_Orders is in hand AND
           Ceruledge_ex is in the active spot AND
           Ceruledge_ex has >= 1 Fire Energy attached AND
           the opponent's active Pokémon HP > damage_count AND
           there exists a Pokémon on the opponent's bench with HP <= damage_count.
Action: PLAY Boss_Orders.
Sub-selection (SWITCH — opponent's benched Pokémon):
    Target the benched Pokémon with the HIGHEST HP that is still <= damage_count.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.23  PLAY EXPLORER'S GUIDANCE  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Explorers_Guidance is in hand AND our deck has > 8 cards.  [anti-deckout]
Action: PLAY Explorers_Guidance.
Sub-selection (TO_HAND from top 6 deck cards — pick exactly 2):
    Score each of the 6 revealed cards using the priority list below.
    Keep the 2 highest-priority cards.

    Priority (1 = highest; first match wins per card, then move to next):
    1.  Brilliant_Blender
    2.  Night_Stretcher
    3.  Carmine — UNLESS another Carmine or Explorers_Guidance is
        already in hand (excluding the one being played now).
    4.  Solrock — IF Lunatone is in play AND no Solrock in play.
    5.  Lunatone — IF Solrock is in play AND no Lunatone in play.
    6.  Poke_Pad — IF exactly one of (Solrock, Lunatone) is in play.
    7.  Fighting_Gong — IF (exactly one of Solrock/Lunatone in play) OR
        (both in play but no Fighting Energy in hand AND Lunar Cycle
        not yet used this turn).
    8.  Explorers_Guidance — UNLESS another Carmine or Explorers_Guidance
        is already in hand.
    9.  Fighting Energy — IF (both Solrock and Lunatone in play AND no
        Fighting Energy in hand AND Lunar Cycle not used this turn) OR
        (the first already-selected card is Lunatone / Solrock /
        Poke_Pad / Fighting_Gong).
    10. Ceruledge_ex — IF a Charcadet in play AND no Ceruledge_ex in
        play AND no Ceruledge_ex already in hand.
    11. Charcadet — IF total (Charcadet + Ceruledge_ex) in play <= 1.
    12. Fire Energy — IF no Fire Energy currently attached to any Pokémon
        AND NOT energyAttached this turn AND no other Fire Energy in hand
        AND (no Night_Stretcher in hand AND Night_Stretcher was NOT
        already selected as the first card from this Explorer's Guidance).
    13. Drilbur
    14. Boss_Orders
    15. Poke_Pad
    16. Fighting_Gong
    17. Ultra_Ball
    18. Ceruledge_ex
    19. Explorers_Guidance
    20. Charcadet
    21. Lunatone
    22. Solrock
    23. Carmine
    24. Fire Energy
    25. Fighting Energy
    26. Any remaining card (random tiebreak)

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.24  PLAY CARMINE  (OptionType.PLAY)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: Carmine is in hand AND our deck has > 8 cards.  [anti-deckout]
Action: PLAY Carmine.
[Carmine's effect is automatic — no sub-selection needed unless the engine
 asks for a discard; discard the lowest-priority cards using the
 Explorer's Guidance priority list in reverse.]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.25  ATTACH ENERGY FROM HAND (late)  (OptionType.ATTACH)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: NOT state.energyAttached AND an energy card is in hand.
Evaluate sub-cases in order, use the first that applies:

  A. IF Charcadet is in the active spot AND it is the first turn
     (state.turn <= 2):
     → Attach Fire Energy to active Charcadet.
        (Never attach Fighting Energy to Charcadet.)

  B. IF a Ceruledge_ex is on the bench with NO energy attached:
     → Attach Fire Energy to that benched Ceruledge_ex.

  C. IF a Charcadet is on the bench with NO energy attached:
     → Attach Fire Energy to that benched Charcadet.
        (Never attach Fighting Energy to Charcadet.)

  D. IF a Solrock is on the bench with NO energy attached:
     → Attach Fighting Energy to that Solrock.
        (Never attach Fire Energy to Solrock.)

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.26  ATTACK  (OptionType.ATTACK)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Condition: An attack option is available.
Action: Use the available attack:
    • If active is Ceruledge_ex → Abyssal Flames  (requires 1 Fire Energy)
    • If active is Solrock      → Cosmic Beam      (requires 1 Fighting Energy;
                                                    only works if Lunatone on bench)
    • If active is Charcadet   → Gather Strength   (takes up to 2 Basic Energy
                                                    from deck into hand; prefer
                                                    1 Fire + 1 Fighting if available)
    • Otherwise                → whichever attack is available

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.27  END TURN  (OptionType.END)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
If no other action above was taken, END the turn.

SECTION 4 — PROMOTE AFTER KO  (SelectContext.TO_ACTIVE after active is KO'd)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Choose replacement in this order:
    1. Ceruledge_ex with Fire Energy attached.
    2. Ceruledge_ex (any, no energy required).
    3. Charcadet with Fire Energy attached AND Ceruledge_ex is in hand.
    4. Charcadet with Ceruledge_ex in hand (no energy on it).
    5. Solrock with Fighting Energy attached AND Lunatone on bench.
    6. Charcadet with any energy attached.
    7. Charcadet (no energy).
    8. Solrock.
    9. Drilbur.
    10. Lunatone.
    11. Any Pokémon.

SECTION 5 — MISC SUB-SELECTIONS (fallback greedy)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
For any context not explicitly covered above
(MULLIGAN YES/NO, IS_FIRST YES/NO, coin flips, etc.):
    Use the greedy fallback (score each option and pick the highest).
