# Historical Ceruledge-RL Material

Files under `old/` are retained for possible future reference. They are not part of the
active runtime, current specification set, test gate, or cluster deployment manifest.

Contents are grouped by why they were retained:

- [`specs/`](specs/) — deferred or superseded specification proposals.
- [`docs/`](docs/) — historical handoffs, summaries, and review reports.
- [`audits/`](audits/) — completed tagging and effect-baking audit bundles.
- [`prototypes/`](prototypes/) — unused experimental implementations.
- [`references/`](references/) — third-party or comparison implementations retained for
  design reference.

Archived Python utilities are not imported by the active runtime. Their import paths
are kept functional where practical so their historical results can be reproduced.
